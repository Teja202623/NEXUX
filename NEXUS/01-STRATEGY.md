**Pillar 1: Educational Authority** (40% of content)

- "5-step framework for local SEO" (methodology)

**Pillar 2: Proof & Results** (30% of content)
- Before/after grid health transformations (visual impact)
- "This client went from 0-40 leads/month in 90 days"

- Customer testimonials in video format
- "Our proprietary grid health scoring system explained"
- Transparent metrics: "Here's how much we spent, here's the ROI"
- **Why:** Social proof closes deals, proves system works, aspirational

**Pillar 3: Industry Secrets/Trends** (20% of content)
- "What we discovered scraping 10,000 competitor ads" (our competitive intel)

- Emerging platforms strategy (new feature launches)
- Market analysis posts: "Local SEO landscape in 2025"
- **Why:** Highly shareable, positions us as insider, viral potential

**Pillar 4: Direct Response & Sales** (10% of content)
- Free audit offers (gated, lead generation)
- "Book a 15-min consultation" CTAs
- Product launches (our new features)
- Limited-time offers
- Webinar invitations
- **Why:** Direct revenue, tests messaging for paid ads

#### **Content Formats (How We'll Present It)**

1. **Carousel Posts** - Multi-slide educational content

4. **Case Study Videos** - Longer-form (2-3 minutes)

5. **Live Content** - Real-time engagement
   - Live Q&A: "Ask us anything about local SEO"

6. **Static Text Posts** - Thought leadership

**Daily Content Generation Pipeline:**

5. **Performance Tracking** (Automated)
   - Daily metrics collection (engagement, reach, saves, shares)
   - Identify top-performing posts
   - A/B test results (which angles, formats, CTAs work)
   - Feed learnings back to content generation
